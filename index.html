How I Work

A Rigorous
Five-Stage Process

01
Initial Briefing
Understanding your objectives, constraints, and the specific challenge — whether evaluation, fundraising, or strategic repositioning.
02
Deep Diligence
Applying institutional-grade scoring matrices to team, market, technology, traction and financial projections against validated benchmarks.
03
Competitive Mapping
Sector landscape analysis — identifying white spaces, competitive moats, and how the opportunity positions within European and global market structures.
04
Strategic Report
A board-ready document detailing findings, risk matrices, value drivers, and a prioritized set of recommendations with clear action ownership.
05
Ongoing Support
Optional ongoing advisory engagement: quarterly check-ins, investor introductions, pitch refinement, and milestone tracking through funding milestones.
